beans with sausages

Tasty and spicy beans with sausages

This is a very tasty recipe that I came up on my own with beans and sausages.

This specific recipe took me about ~6 months to perfect with trying different spices that I had in the kitchen during college.

Ingredients needed for the recipes:

  • 500g Beans
  • Tomato Bouillon
  • sausages
  • A bunch of Parsley
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 big onion or 2 small onions
  • 3 slices of Celery root
  • Chilli/Cayene Chilli Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Secret of Taste from Maggi(Secretul Gustului in romanian) - OPTIONAL

Steps for the Beans:

  1. Place the beans in a medium sized pot
  2. Pour water in the pot untill covering all the beans
  3. Place the pot with beans in the fridge and leave it there for at least 8 hours or overnight
    • The beans will absorb the water and expand
  4. Take the pot out and refill with water untill covering the beans
  5. Put the pot on the Stove
  6. Turn on the stove on medium heat
  7. Leave the pot on medium heat until boiling
  8. While the beans are boiling chop the vegetables
  9. Once the beans boiled, drain all the leftover water
  10. Fill the pot once again with Hot water otherwhise the beans will end up hard instead of soft
  11. Place all the chopped vegetables in the pot with the beans and cover with a lid
  12. Leave the pot on medium heat until the beans are soft when eaten

    13. NOTE:Pour more hot water if needed

Steps for the sausages:

  1. Cut the sausages to the size of a pinky finger
  2. Place them in a frying pan
  3. Sear the sausages for about ~5-10 minutes
  4. Pour the Tomato Bouillon in the pan
  5. Stir the sausages with the Tomato Bouillon for another 5 minutes

Finishing up:

  1. Pour the sausages and Tomato Bouillon in the pot
  2. Stir the beans and the sausages
  3. Add 1/3 Chilli
  4. Add 1/2 Salt
  5. Add 1/3 Pepper
  6. Add 1/4 Secretul Gustului
  7. Leave on medium heat for another 15 minutes
  8. Chop the Parsley while the beans are cooking
  9. Turn off the heat and put the Parsley
  10. ENJOY
